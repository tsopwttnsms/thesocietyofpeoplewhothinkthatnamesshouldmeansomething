Welcome to The Society of People who Think that Names Should Mean Something

What is our goal?
Simple: bring together those with the view that names should describe the thing that they are naming. Below you will find many examples of organizations that have failed at this task.

How do I join?

There are a few primary meeting places for the society. First of all, there is the subreddit. In addition, if you have any suggestions for this website, you can reach out at tsopwttnsms@gmail.com. In addition, you can join the discord here.
